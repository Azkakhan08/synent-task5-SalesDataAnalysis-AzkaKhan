# Task 5 - Sales Data Analysis

## Problem Statement
A retail business wants to understand its sales performance. Which months have highest revenue? Which products sell most? Which regions generate most profit? This task analyzes Superstore sales data to answer these business questions.

---

## Dataset Details

 Field        |          Details                      
---------------------------------------
 Dataset Name   |   Superstore Sales Dataset     
 Data         |    12 months  10 products  4 regions 
 Source       |    Defined inside the code      

 Column Name        |        Description                   
-----------------------------------------
 month       |   Month name Jan to Dec         
 revenue     |  Total revenue for that month  
 profit      |       Total profit for that month   
 product     |  Product name                  
 sales       |  Total sales amount            
 region      |  Region name                   

---

## Approach

Step 1 - Define three datasets monthly products and regions

Step 2 - Calculate total revenue and total profit

Step 3 - Find best month and worst month

Step 4 - Rank top 3 and top 5 products by sales

Step 5 - Create Bar Chart for monthly revenue

Step 6 - Create Line Chart for monthly profit trend

Step 7 - Create Horizontal Bar Chart for top 5 products

Step 8 - Create Bar Chart for profit by region
